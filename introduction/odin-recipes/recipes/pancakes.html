🥞 American Pancakes

Description

Fluffy American-style pancakes perfect for breakfast.

🍓 Ingredients

  • 1 cup of flour
  • 1 tablespoon of sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 cup of milk
  • 2 tablespoons of melted butter

👩🍳 Preparation

  1. Mix dry ingredients
  2. Add liquids and mix
  3. Cook on a hot skillet
  4. Serve with syrup or fruits
