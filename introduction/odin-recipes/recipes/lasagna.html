🍝 Classic Lasagna

Description

A delicious traditional Italian lasagna with layers of pasta, meat, béchamel sauce, and melted cheese.

🍅 Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna pasta sheets
  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 800g tomato sauce
  • 500ml béchamel sauce
  • 300g mozzarella cheese

👩🍳 Preparation

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C
  2. Brown the meat with the onion
  3. Alternate layers of pasta, meat, sauces, and cheese
  4. Bake for 40 minutes
